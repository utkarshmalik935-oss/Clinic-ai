# WhatsApp Templates for Clinic Workflow

1) Appointment request confirmation (user asks to book)
Hi {name}, thanks for contacting {clinic_name}. I can help you book an appointment. Which doctor do you prefer and what time suits you?

2) Appointment confirmation (after slot assigned)
Hi {name}, your appointment with {doctor} is confirmed for {date_time}. Please arrive 10 minutes early. Reply 'cancel' to cancel.

3) Reminder (24 hours before)
Reminder: Hi {name}, this is a reminder for your appointment with {doctor} on {date_time}. Reply 'reschedule' to change.

4) Report upload instruction
Hi {name}, please upload your lab report here: {upload_link}. We'll review and send a summary to your doctor within 24 hours.

5) Report processed
Hi {name}, we've processed your report. Summary: {short_summary}. If you'd like to discuss, reply 'call' to schedule a teleconsultation.
